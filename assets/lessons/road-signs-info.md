## Information Signs

Information signs are **rectangular** with a **blue background**. They provide useful information to drivers such as directions, services, and facilities.

### Key features of information signs:

- **Shape**: Rectangular (usually)
- **Background**: Blue
- **Border**: White
- **Purpose**: Provide information and directions

### Types of information signs:

1. **Direction Signs** - Guide you to destinations
2. **Service Signs** - Indicate amenities (fuel, food, rest areas)
3. **Tourist Information Signs** - Point to attractions
4. **Car Park Signs** - Show parking locations
5. **Lane Control Signs** - Indicate lane usage

### Examples include:

- Restaurant symbol (knife & fork)
- Hospital symbol (red cross)
- Fuel station symbol (petrol pump)
- Accommodation symbol (bed)

### Tips for using information signs:

- Read them in advance to plan your route
- Don't stop suddenly to read signs
- Be aware of upcoming services and facilities
- Use them to find alternative routes if needed

Remember: Information signs help you navigate and find services you may need during your journey.
