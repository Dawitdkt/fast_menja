## Warning Signs

Warning signs are **triangular** with a **red border** and a yellow background. They warn drivers of hazards ahead.

### Key features of warning signs:

- **Shape**: Triangular
- **Background**: Yellow
- **Border**: Red
- **Purpose**: Alert drivers to potential hazards

### Common warning signs include:

1. **Skid Warning** - Indicates slippery roads
2. **Children Crossing** - Warns of areas with children
3. **Pedestrian Crossing** - Indicates crossing points
4. **Cycle Crossing** - Warning for cyclists
5. **Animal Crossing** - Wildlife may cross the road

### Tips for recognizing warning signs:

- Always watch for triangular signs with red borders
- Anticipate hazards mentioned on the sign
- Adjust your speed and be prepared to stop
- Pay special attention in residential areas and near schools

Remember: Warning signs require you to be alert and ready to change your driving depending on the conditions.
